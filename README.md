# Zomato-Restaurant-Analytics-Dashboard-Customer-Behavior-Business-Performance

**Description**
An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes restaurant performance, customer ratings, pricing trends, and location-wise insights to help businesses improve service quality and increase revenue.

**Tech Stack**
Power BI Desktop – Data visualization & dashboard creation
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures and calculated columns
Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data format handling
Data Source: Data provided via Inttruv

**Data Source**
The dataset used for this project is sourced from Inttruv, containing restaurant-level data such as location, ratings, cost, cuisines, and customer preferences.

**Features & Highlights of the Project**
Business Problem
Food delivery platforms and restaurant owners struggle to understand:
Which locations perform best
What price range customers prefer
Which cuisines drive higher ratings and demand
How ratings impact business visibility and revenue

**Goal of the Dashboard**
To provide a data-driven decision tool that helps stakeholders:
Identify high-performing locations and cuisines
Optimize pricing strategies
Improve customer satisfaction based on rating analysis
Support expansion and marketing decisions

**Key Visuals Walkthrough**

City-wise Restaurant Distribution Map – Shows concentration of restaurants by location
Average Rating Analysis – Performance comparison across cities/cuisines
Cost for Two Analysis (Bar/Histogram) – Identifies popular price ranges
Cuisine-wise Popularity Chart – Most demanded food categories
Online Delivery/Table Booking Insights – Customer service preference trends
Top Restaurants Table – High-rated and premium performers
Filters/Slicers – City, Cuisine, Price Range, Rating

**Business Impact & Insights**
Identifies high-demand cuisines for expansion
Helps restaurants optimize pricing based on customer preference
Improves customer experience by focusing on factors affecting ratings
Supports location-based investment decision
Enables food platforms to target marketing campaigns effectively
